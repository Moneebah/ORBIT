FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

ORBIT is a 3D interactive platform that simplifies investment decisions by providing comprehensive global economic insights in one accessible location.

How do I access the 3D globe?
+

You can access the 3D globe by logging into the ORBIT platform and clicking on the “Explore” tab.

How do I view investment data for a specific country?
+

To view investment data for a specific country, click on the country on the 3D globe. A dashboard will appear showcasing vital economic data and investment opportunities in the specified country.

Can I customize the data displayed on the dashboard?
+

Yes, you can customize the data displayed on the dashboard by filtering and sorting according to your preferences.

Where does ORBIT get its data from?
+

ORBIT uses the most authentic datasets from sources such as the World Bank API to provide accurate and up-to-date information.

How often is the data updated?
+

The frequency of data updates depends on the source of the data. For example, data from the World Bank API is updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Can I suggest additional data sources for ORBIT to use?
+

Yes, we welcome suggestions for additional data sources. Please contact us with your suggestions.

How does ORBIT protect my personal information?
+

ORBIT takes the security and privacy of your personal information very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.

Does ORBIT share my personal information with third parties?
+

No, ORBIT does not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

What should I do if my account has been compromised?
+

If you suspect that your account has been compromised, please contact our support team immediately for assistance.
